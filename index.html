What is GERMUN ?

GERMUN is the most prestigious Model United Conference in Morocco. A Model

United Nations conference is one in which students from all over the world

take the role of an international delegate of any given country. These delegations

join various committees in order to debate current or recent worldwide issues,

with the purpose of finding viable solutions, much like how the real United Nations

committees operate.
