SOPHIE GERMAIN MODEL UNITED NATIONS 2023

February 2023

The Lycée français Sophie Germain invites you to its Model United Nations (MUN).

To beheld in January 2024, this conference is the ideal place to practice

the tools of diplomacy and international relations,

and is open to all high school and university students aged 14 to 21.

We will have a variety of delegates from Morocco and the world taking part

in our 23 committees. As always, the health and safety of our staff and

delegates continues to be a priority.

GERMUN ...

Hundreds of thousands of students from all over the world take part every year, at all levels of education.

Our school, the Lycée français sophie Germain, offers us and you, the participants from all over the world,

an opportunity to debate a wide range of subjects while acting as ambassadors.

Tackling topical issues to familiarize today's youth with their socio-economic and political environment and enable them to master their future
