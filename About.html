SOPHIE GERMAIN MODEL UNITED NATIONS 2023

February 2023

The Lycée français Sophie Germain invites you to its Model United Nations (MUN).

To beheld in January 2024, this conference is the ideal place to practice

the tools of diplomacy and international relations,

and is open to all high school and university students aged 14 to 21.

We will have a variety of delegates from Morocco and the world taking part

in our 23 committees. As always, the health and safety of our staff and

delegates continues to be a priority.

an opportunity to debate a wide range of subjects while acting as ambassadors.

The MUN, training for future national and international decision-makers and negotiators.

The MUN offers hands-on experience of diplomacy by simulating the role of a diplomat from

a specific country, giving an insight into the workings of international relations and diplomacy.

By taking part in debates and negotiations, delegates gain confidence in their ability to

express their ideas and defend their position in front of an audience.

The MUN is an enriching educational experience, offering participants valuable skills and

knowledge for their personal and professional growth.

Learning about world affairs:

Participants acquire in-depth competence in current global games, international politics, relations between nations and complex international problems.

Communication skills development:

Delegates improve their communication skills by learning to express their ideas clearly, defend their positions and negotiate with other participants.

Strengthening problem-solving skills:

participants learn to solve complex problems and establish effective solutions by working as a team to draft resolutions that address crucial global issues.

Developing leadership skills:

participants have the opportunity to lead debates, facilitate discussions and encourage collaboration between delegates, thereby strengthening their leadership skills.
